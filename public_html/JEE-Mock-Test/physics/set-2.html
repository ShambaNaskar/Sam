A.

Hertz

B.
de Broglie
C.
Davisson and Germer
D.
Einstein
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option C

2.

A satellite is moving with a constant speed v in a circular orbit about the earth. An object of mass m is ejected from the satellite such that it just escapes from the gravitational pull of the earth. At the time of its ejection, the kinetic energy of the object is

A.

(3/2)mv2

B.
0.5mv2
C.
mv2
D.
2mv2
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option C

3.

A seconds pendulum is mounted in a rocket. Its period of oscillation decreases when the rocket __________.

A.

moves up with uniform acceleration

B.
moves up with a uniform velocity
C.
comes down with uniform acceleration
D.
moves round the earth in a geostationary orbit
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option A

A.

0.1 Tesla

B.
0.2 Tesla
C.
0.8 Tesla
D.
1.6 Tesla
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option B

5.

Two moles of ideal helium gas are in a rubber balloon at 30°C. The balloon is fully expandable and can be assumed to require no energy in its expansion. The temperature of the gas in the balloon is slowly changed to 35°C. The amount of heat required in raising the temperature is nearly (take R = 8.31 J/mol-K)

A.

104 J

B.
208 J
C.
124 J
D.
62 J
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option B

B.
2.5 A
C.
25.1 A
D.
1.7 A

1.41 × 10-8

0.70 × 10-8

4.23 × 10-8

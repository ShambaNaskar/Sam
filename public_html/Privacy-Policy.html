Privacy Policy

  • Limited Data Collection:

    ScienceLesson only collects personal information, such as name and email address, from users who voluntarily opt to join our affordable live classes. We do not gather data from regular website visitors.

  • Information Usage:

    Collected data is solely used to facilitate enrollment in our live classes, manage subscriptions, and provide educational services. We do not share this information with any third parties.

  • Data Protection:

    We employ stringent security measures to protect the limited user information we collect, ensuring it is safeguarded against unauthorized access or misuse.

  • Cookie Usage:

    Our website might use cookies solely for essential functionalities like session management. Users can manage cookie preferences through browser settings.

  • Children's Privacy:

    ScienceLesson does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years old. Parental consent is required for minors to enroll in our services.

  • Data Retention:

    User data collected for enrollment purposes is retained only for the duration necessary to provide the requested educational services. Users can request data deletion or corrections if needed.

  • Policy Updates:

    This privacy policy may be updated periodically. Users will be informed of any changes, and continued use of the website implies acceptance of the revised policy.

  • Contact Information:

    Users can reach out to us for privacy-related concerns, data queries, or to exercise their rights under this policy using the provided contact information. Please contact us at mail@sciencelesson.in for any further assistance regarding privacy matters.

Electrostatics

Test set 2

Full marks: 40

Time: 10 Minutes

Each right answer contains +4 and Wrong answer -1 marks.

Time remaining: 10:00

1.

Three capacitors each of capacitance C and of breakdown voltage V are joined in series. The capacitance and breakdown voltage of the combination will be: AIPMT 2009

A.

C/3, 3V

B.
3C, V/3
C.
C/3, V/3
D.
3C, 3V
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option A

2.

A parallel plate air capacitor is charged to a potential difference of V volts. After disconnecting the charging battery the distance between the plates of the capacitor is increased using an insulating handle. As a result the potential difference between the plates __________. CBSE AIPMT 2006

A.

decreases

B.
increases
C.
does not change
D.
becomes zero
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option B

3.

Two small spheres each carrying a charge q are placed r metre apart. If one of the spheres is taken around the other one in a circular path of radius r, the work done will be equal to: CPMT 91, 2001

A.

Force between them × 2πr

B.
Force between them × r
C.
Zero
D.
Force between them /2πr
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option C

4.

A simple pendulum of period T has a metal bob which is negatively charged. If it is allowed to oscillate above a positively charged metal plate, its period will: CBSE PMT 2001

A.

Infinite

B.
Remains equal to T
C.
Greater than T
D.
Less than T
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option D

5.

Three capacitors each of capacity 4 µF are to be connected in such a way that the effective capacitance is 6 µF. This can be done by __________. CBSE AIPMT 2003

A.

connecting two in parallel and one in series

B.
connecting all of them in parallel
C.
connecting two in series and one in parallel
D.
connecting all of them in series
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option C

6.

An electric dipole is kept in non - uniform electric field. It experiences __________.

A.

A force but not a torque

B.
A force and a torque
C.
Neither a force nor a torque
D.
A torque but not a force
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option B

7.

Some charge is being given to a conductor, then its potential is CBSE AIPMT 2002

A.

same throughout the conductor

B.
maximum at surface
C.
maximum somewhere between surface and centre
D.
maximum at centre
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option A

8.

When one electron is taken towards the other electron, then the electric potential energy of the system __________. CBSE PMT 1993

A.

Increases

B.
Decreases
C.
Becomes zero
D.
Remains unchanged
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option A

9.

The formation of a dipole is due to two equal and dissimilar point charges placed at a __________.CBSE AIPMT 1996

A.

long distance

B.
short distance
C.
above each other
D.
None of these
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option B

10.

When a proton is accelerated through 1 V, then its kinetic energy will be: CBSE PMT 1999

A.

0.54 eV

B.
13.6 eV
C.
1840 eV
D.
1 eV
Ans. A B C D
Correct answer: Option D

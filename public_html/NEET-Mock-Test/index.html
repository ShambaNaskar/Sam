FAQs on Online NEET Mock Tests

  • Q:- What is NEET?

    A:- NEET is a national-level entrance examination in India conducted for admission to undergraduate medical and dental courses, including MBBS and BDS, in various government and private colleges across the country.

  • Q:- Who conducts the NEET exam?

    A:- The National Testing Agency (NTA) is responsible for conducting the NEET exam on behalf of the Ministry of Health and Family Welfare, Government of India.

  • Q:- What is the eligibility criteria for NEET?

    A:- To be eligible for NEET, a candidate must have completed 10+2 or its equivalent with Physics, Chemistry, Biology/Biotechnology, and English as core subjects. They must also meet the age and nationality requirements.

  • Q:- What is the age limit for NEET?

    A:- As of my last knowledge update in September 2021, the minimum age for NEET eligibility is 17 years, and there is no upper age limit for the exam. However, please check the latest information as age limits may change.

  • Q:- How many times can I attempt the NEET exam?

    A:- There is no limit on the number of attempts for NEET, subject to meeting the age and other eligibility criteria.

  • Q:- What is the syllabus for NEET?

    A:- The NEET syllabus includes topics from Physics, Chemistry, and Biology (Botany and Zoology) based on the 11th and 12th-grade NCERT curriculum.

  • Q:- How is the NEET exam structured?

    A:- NEET is a pen-and-paper-based exam consisting of 180 multiple-choice questions. It comprises 45 questions each from Physics and Chemistry, and 90 questions from Biology (45 from Botany and 45 from Zoology). The exam duration is 3 hours.

  • Q:- Is there negative marking in NEET?

    A:- Yes, there is a negative marking system in NEET. For each incorrect answer, one mark is deducted, while four marks are awarded for a correct answer.

  • Q:- What is the NEET All India Rank (AIR)?

    A:- The NEET All India Rank (AIR) is the rank obtained by a candidate in the national-level merit list. It is used for admission to medical and dental colleges across India.
